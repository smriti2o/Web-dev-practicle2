CCET(DG): 4 on-campus beverages you should be drinking

Smriti Kumari

Last updated 26 February 2022

  • CURIOUS ELIXIRS

    Hotel Aquamarine, Adjacemt Aroma Hotel and Restaurant, Sector 22, Chandigarh

    This non-alcoholic drink brand offers bottle mocktails inspired by their boozy counterparts, and while they aren't exact matches per se, they're tasty in their own right. Take Elixir No. 1 for example: It's described as a “beautifully bitter booze-free pomegranate cocktail inspired by the Negroni, infused with rhodiola to lift you up,” with a tartness that's unexpected but nonetheless tasty.

  • Iced tea

    Elante Mall, Industrial Area, Phase 1, Chandigarh

    Iced tea is a form of cold tea. Though usually served in a glass with ice, it can refer to any tea that has been chilled or cooled. It may be sweetened with sugar, syrup and/or apple slices. Iced tea is also a popular packaged drink and can be mixed with flavored syrup, with multiple common flavors including lemon, raspberry, lime, passion fruit, peach, orange, strawberry, and cherry.

  • Mint Oreo Milkshake

    Uncle Jack's 8B, Sector 8, Chandigarh

    Mint Oreo Milkshake is a perfect no bake dessert. It is so easy the kids can make it themselves - only 3 ingredients! Refreshingly quick and easy for a summer treat

  • Mocha

    Hotel Aquamarine, Adjacemt Aroma Hotel and Restaurant, Sector 22, Chandigarh

    Mocha is an intensely flavorful coffee blend. It's an espresso-based drink that has the same ingredients of your favorite coffee drinks, such as the cappuccino and latte, which contains steamed milk, a shot or two of espresso, and chocolate. The mocha is very closely related to the classic latte, which began to surge in popularity in the 1980s in the United States.
